Scene 0: The Prologue
-
- **Background: A high-school, with graduating students at the background**

*(The students cheering and celebrating after graduation)*

**Isaac's Aunt**: I'll be at the comfort room for now, can you wait me at the student lounge, Isaac?

**Isaac**: Sure, auntie, take your time.

**Isaac**: "We're more than ready, the world's in our hands..."

**Isaac**: "A new beginning, it's time to stand..."

**Isaac**: *That is the part of the chorus of our graduation song earlier. More than ready, huh... I never felt ready for the road ahead.*

*High school, what a ride... Studies, careers, being someone that only focuses on his tasks at hand. High school's all about that for me. Hmm, funny enough... I don't think I have actually feel in love with someone... or maybe I never let myself to. Besides, the field that I wanted will risk anyone who's around me. Having someone close to me to be in danger because of me, that's something I can't handle with my conscience...*

*Enough thinking! Focus, Isaac. Now that I am already graduated here, and the fact the I passed to the program that I wanted in my hometown's state university, I guess that's what I'm gonna do for now. Just go with the flow, see where this road leads us.*

**Isaac's Aunt**: I'm back, Isaac! Are you ready with your college life?

**Isaac**: Yeah. But there is some unfinished business I must do before that, though.

**Isaac's Aunt**: Oh, is that about when your school cancelled all trainings and certifications for your specialization? You still plan on taking it there?

**Isaac**: Yeah, my sister's boyfriend told me their college currently offers free electrical training and certification, plus there is an allowance once the training is complete. I wouldn't want to miss that one though, right? *laughs*

**Isaac's Aunt**: That's right, Isaac! I'm glad you seized that opportunity. I hope you had a great time in our hometown. Your parents haven't seen you for months after your last vacation there.

**Isaac**: I really hope to enjoy it. High school has been a lot for me. What more can college life, especially engineering give me... But it's like you said, no pressure, go with the flow.

**Isaac's Aunt**: Yes, Isaac. No pressure. Live your life to the fullest, do things that makes you happy as long as it is for good. No matter what, I'll always support you! :) 

**Isaac**: Thank you, auntie... *genuine smile*

**Isaac**: *My aunt's always been supportive of me and to my cousins. But having that trust and support... It still gave me the pressure. The pressure to not fail this and to never fail them...*


Scene 1: A New Beginning
-
*(Background: The electrical training room)*

*One month later, at Isaac's hometown*

**Isaac**: (inspecting the equipments)

**Isaac**: *Those hardwares... pliers, screwdrivers, even those MCPs and CCTVs. These all bring back memories. But man, what the hell. Training us for 2 whole senior high school years, only to discontinue the goddamned certification exam! Still pissed for the department for that* (sighs)  


**Erick (sister's boyfriend)**: What's up, yo? You look like you got something in mind.

**Isaac**: Just nostalgia, *kuya*. (laughs) I'm already liking this laboratory, though. You said you also trained here?

**Erick**: Yep! Though I mostly struggled in CCTVs, I still did fine. But hey, you'll manage great here. Especially since you're the "leader" and the brain in your EIM classes in senior high, right? (laughs)

**Isaac**: Hey, I'm just a leader on research! My skills are slight inferior compared to my classmates--

**Erick**: Oh, Sir Lex is here... you can now go to your seat there. Good luck! See you later in luch, bro!

**Isaac**: Alright, Kuya. See you later!

*(the room settled down)*

**Sir Lex**: Welcome to our Electrical Installation and Maintenance training! I am Sir Lex, and I will be your trainer for this month. I hope we will have a great time! Now, let's introduce yourselves!

*(talking in the background)*

**Isaac**: *Sir Lex, only based on his appearance and speaking, he really is a professional, yet chill and friendly. I am already liking this training.*

*Also, those fellows, most of them looks a lot older than me. Some would look like they already have their own family or are working. I feel like there are only few young ones like me... this is awkward. I hate being awkward.*

*(a few minutes later)*

**Isaac**: *What the hell? A criminology graduate, third year in architecture, a technician, and IT grad inside this room? Along with some EE grads and students older than me... As if finding a friend the same age as me in this city was already hard enough...*

**Sir Lex**: Up next, Cynthia!

**Cynthia**: (Stands up) Uhm... Good morning, sir Lex, and good morning to everyone, my name is Cynthia, 18 years old, just graduated at high school under STEM, and umm... I don't have any experience with electrical yet... and what I, uh, eexpect with this class... is only to learn more about EIM... Thank you...!

**Isaac**: *Cynthia... the same batch as me, finally. But yeah, not possible. She already has her own circle of friends. Oh yeah, when kuya Erick added me as the first student in our group chat, she's one of the first person that I've seen added after me. Seems someone who is low-key... I wasn't trying to pry, but I just wanted to know who am I gonna be with for a month. I have always been that paranoid and curious. I even checked the profiles of others; grads and working. That's why she's one of the first people that took my curiosity. She looks like someone that I might be close to because of age...*

*continuation*

**Sir Lex**: ...Thank you for your introduction! Now, next up on the list is Isaac!

**Isaac**: *Alright, here we go...* *(stands up)*

**Isaac**: Good morning, sir Lex and to everyone... I am Isaac, an 18 year-old student, graduated at a science-technology high-school. And I look forward to train with everyone! *Bruh... was that too lame of an intro? I only aim to speak less so that it won't feel like I'm boasting...*

**Sir Lex**: ...do you have any experience with electrical?

**Isaac**: *Ah shit, I forgot about that...* ... Oh yeah, I graduated in high school with the specialization about EIM, along with EPAS before that. I also had experience with an OJT under the Institute of Electrical Engineering.

**Sir Lex**: Oh, someone that already has experience with EIM, as well as Electronics? That's cool! But why do you want to take this training, Isaac?

**Isaac**: To continue my training for EIM's certification exam, sir. My high school cancelled that exam to give time for our OJT.

**Sir Lex**: I see. I hope you can still enjoy training, Isaac. I look forward to have you as one of our trainees!

**Isaac**: Thank you sir. *(sits down)* *Goddamnit... confidence with awkwardness, I hate that. Always kept on overthinking what I wanted to say, and then becomes tangled, some forgotten. Welp, at least that was all done.*

*(few minutes later)*

**Sir Lex**: For this election, who would you like to be president of this class?

*(awkward silence)*

...

**Isaac**: *Great, another awkward silence. Election has always been this rigged. It makes the whole room in order, sure, but still...* 

**Jess**: (Raises hand)

**Sir Lex**: Yes, Jess?

**Jess**: I respectfully nominate... Isaac! (points at Isaac)

**Isaac**: (shocked) What?! (speechless)

(laughter)

**Sir Lex**: Isaac is now listed. Any other nominees?

(silence)

**Sir Lex**: Alright, with that, Isaac is now holding the position of the president!

**Isaac**: *As if my time here in this town will get any better... Jess: she's with Cynthia, right? Same school, same batch. Unlike Cynthia, she seems more outgoing and social. Well, that was obvious due to the fact that out of all the people here, she nominated me. Man, what a hassle...*

*(after election)*

**Sir Lex**: Now that the officers are now elected, let's now proceed on our first lesson...

**Isaac**: *Now here comes the fun part. I actually expect that these are the things I have already learned, but then again, a review will be too helpful for me. Let's see where this training goes for me...*
