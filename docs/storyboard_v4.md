
# Summer Circuits – Storyboard (Revised)

## Story Overview

**Summer Circuits** follows Isaac, an 18-year-old recent high school graduate who spends his summer attending an Electrical Installation and Maintenance (EIM) training before entering college.

Expecting nothing more than technical lessons, he instead finds himself gradually becoming part of a group of people who quietly reshape his outlook on friendship, confidence, and love.

The story is told through **Isaac's inner thoughts**, allowing the player to experience both the conversations happening around him and the insecurities he keeps to himself.

---

# Narrative Philosophy

- Romance grows naturally from friendship.
- Ordinary moments matter more than dramatic events.
- Characters are allowed to misunderstand one another.
- First impressions evolve over time.
- Isaac's personal growth is as important as the romance.

---

# Chapter Structure

## Chapter 0 — Prologue
**Goal:** Introduce Isaac's worldview after graduation and establish his fears about the future and relationships.

## Chapter 1 — A New Beginning
**Goal:** Introduce the EIM training, Sir Lex, and the trainees. Establish Isaac's first impressions of Cynthia and the class.

## Chapter 2 — Drafts
**Goal:** Cynthia asks Isaac for help with wiring schematics, breaking the ice between them.

## Chapter 3 — Inner Conflicts
**Goal:** Explore Isaac's insecurity as he watches other trainees socialize.

### Key Events
- Isaac notices Cynthia and Jess becoming comfortable with the class.
- Feeling isolated, he begins overthinking whether he truly belongs.
- To distract himself, he calls **Michael**, his senior high school friend and former EIM classmate.
- Isaac gives Michael a short tour of the laboratory and they reminisce about their old EIM days.
- The conversation briefly eases Isaac's mind before he returns to class.
- Isaac realizes he can't stay attached only to the past—he has to allow himself to build new friendships.

## Chapter 4 — The Mall
**Goal:** Group outing initiated by Rena. Isaac's passion for photography becomes a major bonding point with Cynthia.

## Chapter 5 — Closure
**Goal:** Isaac and Cynthia chat from their respective homes, learning more about each other. Cynthia playfully ships Isaac with Jean.

## Chapter 6 — Enrollment Blues
**Goal:** Isaac supports Cynthia through enrollment difficulties, strengthening their trust.

## Chapter 7 — Summer Circuits
**Goal:** Cynthia visits Isaac's house for EIM review. The confession and branching endings occur here.

---

# Training Class Dynamics

- Every trainee is attending Sir Lex's EIM training **for the first time**.
- Nobody personally knows Sir Lex before Chapter 1.
- The trainees come from different educational and professional backgrounds.
- Winston and Ray are already close friends because they are classmates in their third year of Electrical Engineering.
- Cynthia and Jess already know each other from senior high school.
- Michael does **not** attend the training; he appears during Chapter 3 through a phone/video call.

---

# Core Themes

- Self-worth
- First impressions
- Friendship before romance
- Personal growth
- Transition into adulthood
- Photography as preserving memories
- Circuits as a metaphor for human connection

---

# Ending Structure

Chapter 7 concludes with one major decision leading to four endings.
Three endings are alternate "what if" outcomes, while the canon ending concludes the story and represents Isaac's completed emotional journey.
